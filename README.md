# Disney Bounding Catalog

**Disney Bounding** is the hobby of dressing up in the same colors as famous Disney characters.

This website is a catalog of various Disney characters, providing guidance for what colors to look out for when shopping for clothes.

* Phase 1: People can search for their favorite characters and see what colors they should shop for.
* Phase 2: People can upload images of their outfits to certain characters in order to provide inspiration to others.
* Possible future phases: Seeing actual product recommendations from online stores, liking each other's images, commenting and discussion, etc.

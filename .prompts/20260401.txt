After thinking about it, I want to reorganize character outfits. Instead of a one-to-many relationship of one character having many outfits, I want each character to outfit to actually be a separate "character". The reason is so from the home page, when someone searches "Ariel" they can see all of Ariel's different outfits right then and there, and if they search by color then they will get the variations of Ariel that include that color rather than just Ariel in general. Can you refactor the data models to this new paradigm?

Many characters are only going to have one outfit. I think it's acceptable for the "default" or only outfit of characters to simply not have an outfit name. So for Ariel, the "mermaid" outfit would be the default, so that outfit will not have a name, and the slug will be just "ariel". Can you change the character data accordingly?
